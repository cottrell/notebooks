See here

Nothing here, just some mathjax and how to get sensitivities the wrong way, at least if varying \(X\) means changing it and not simply being exposed to or observing it.

$$ \begin{align} Y &\sim F_{A}(X) + \epsilon \\ Y - F_{A}(X) &\sim G_{B}(X, X') + \eta \\ \end{align} $$
And then you can naively extract information about the sensativities or importances by perturbing:
$$ g_{X'}(X) = \partial_{X'} G_B(X, X') $$

In-line math is also possible \(\int_{t=0}^{\infty} f(t) \text{d}t\).
